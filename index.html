Welcome to Unit 318

Everything you need to get settled in

🅿️ Request a Parking Pass

We're so glad you're here. Below you'll find step-by-step directions from the parking garage to your front door, building amenities, and our favorite local spots. If you need anything at all, don't hesitate to reach out.

Arrival & Check-In
🅿️
Parking
  • When you arrive, you'll see two parking garages. Enter the second garage — it's called Tower Garage.
  • Drive down the ramp and make two immediate right turns.
  • You'll see the elevator lobby on your left side, labeled Tower Elevators B3–7.
Parking Note: Complimentary parking is available for stays of 3 nights or more. Request your parking pass here →
🛗
Getting Upstairs
  • Take the Tower Elevator up to Floor 3.
  • Exit the elevator and turn left.
  • Walk down the hallway, then make a right.
  • Unit 318 will be on the right-hand side of the hallway.
🔑
Getting Inside
  • At the door, you'll find a digital keypad lock box.
  • Enter the PIN code provided in your booking details to open the lock box.
  • Inside the lock box is a key fob — grab it and keep it with you during your stay.
Important: The key fob is how you'll get in and out of the building, including all common areas and amenities. Always bring it with you!
Navigating the Building
🏢
Main Elevator
  • Walk right out of Unit 318.
  • Make your first left.
  • Follow that walkway to the Main Elevator.
🛗
Tower Elevator
  • Walk out of Unit 318 and turn left.
  • Head down the hallway — you'll see a door on the left side. It's usually propped open.
  • If the door is closed, look for the door with no label — that's the one.
  • Go through it and you'll reach the Tower Elevator.
Amenities
🏊
Main Pool
  • Head to the Tower Elevator and go to Floor 2.
  • Exit the elevator and turn left.
  • When you get outside, turn left again and follow the path.
  • You'll find the main pool ahead.
Tip: Bring your key fob and grab beach towels from the cabinet in the unit. The pool area has showers and bathrooms available.
🎳
Arcade, Bowling & Game Room
  • Head to the Main Elevator and take it to Floor 1.
  • Exit the elevator — the game room will be on your right side.
  • Remember to bring your key fob.
Bonus: All the games are completely free to play!
💪
Gym
  • Head to the Tower Elevator and go to Floor 2.
  • Exit the elevator and turn left.
  • When you get outside, turn left — the gym will be right in front of you.
💼
Business Center
  • Head to the Tower Elevator and go to Floor 2.
  • Exit the elevator and turn left.
  • When you get outside, turn right and walk past the gym.
  • Continue through the opening between the buildings.
  • When you reach the next building, you'll see a walkway inside. Head down that hallway.
  • The Business Center will be on the right.
Getting to Petco Park
⚾
Petco Park
  • The fastest way to Petco Park is the stairs right next to Unit 318.
  • Head down the stairs and you're practically there!
Heads up: The stairs are one-way only — you cannot use them to get back into the building.

Getting back inside:

  • Walk past Honey Hole (the card shop).
  • You'll see a glass door — use your key fob to open it.
  • This brings you to the Tower Elevator, and you're home.
